Only A Real Gamer Can Name These Awesome Video Games

While older games like Space Travel claim to be the world's first video game, only one can be considered the world's first "popular video game" and that is Pong. Which really was just a simple table tennis game. Kids these days could never sit and play Pong for as long as 70s kids did. But that's because times are changing and so are video games. That's why today's quiz will be on modern video games that anyone who is anyone will recognize. Or at least anyone who is anyone in the gaming community.
But before we start, let's talk about popularity. While Pong was the first popular game, only one can be the most popular. And that game is Tetris, with over 170 million downloads/purchases, the game has broken records that can hard ever be broken again. And has also inspired thousands of other games in the process. The closest in line is one game that is still 30 million away from breaking it. And that's with numbers rising on them both every year. Hopefully, one day a game will be released that surpasses Tetris, but until then we're met with amazing games with merely tens of millions of downloads or purchases.
What we're going to do is give you a screenshot and a short description and you'll tell us what video game comes to mind. Don't worry, there's no time limit so take it easy and enjoy! Bet you can name at least half of them if you just put your mind to it!
